About Us

Cipta Inova System is a company that provides HVAC control and building automation systems in Indonesia. This company was founded in 2020 and had revenues of IDR 23,962,500,000 (Model) in 2016. The company also offers measuring, monitoring, regulation and detection tools. The company has imported various products from Sweden, China and others, such as water/air flow sensors, pressure transmitters, differential pressure controllers and more.
